# Product Overview

This is a personal work-in-progress (WIP) repository that serves as a centralized task and project management system. The repository follows "The Inbox Method" workflow for capturing, processing, and routing tasks across multiple business and personal projects.

## Key Projects
- **HWPC**: Hardware/PC business operations and customer management
- **LLC End of Year**: Business accounting and year-end processes  
- **AI CLI Research**: Technical research into AI command-line tools
- **Computer Kits**: Hardware assembly and configuration projects
- **Personal Tasks**: Vehicle repairs, administrative tasks, and personal projects

## Primary Purpose
The repository acts as a central hub for:
- Task capture and management using a structured workflow
- Research documentation and link archival
- Project coordination across multiple business entities
- Personal productivity tracking with metrics and scheduling
